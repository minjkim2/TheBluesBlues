ROCK MUSIC

Rock Music has been around for a while now, it originated as rock and roll during the late 1940s and the beginning of 1950, and in the 1960s it developed into different styles of Rock Music. Rock Music wasn't really created by one person, it was a black and white alloy of Fats Domino, Lloyd Price, Ike Turner, Hank Williams, Joe Turner, Louis Jordan, Ray Charles, Little Richard, Jerry Lee Lewis, Chuck Berry, Bo Diddley, Buddy Holly, and Elvis Presley. It is known that Rock Music originated and evolved in the United states. There are different types of style of rock music some are Acid rock,Art punk, Bandana Thrash, Big beat, Blues-rock, Death rock, Instrumental rock, Metalcore, Taqwacore, etc.
