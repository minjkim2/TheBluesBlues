CUBISM

Who: Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque are credited with beginning cubism.

What: Objects are drawn or painted from many angles and viewpoints in the same piece of art.

When: Early 20th Century (1907 - 1922)

Where: France (Paris)

Why: The multiple viewpoints show more about the subjects' overall "personality than a one perspective painting would.
