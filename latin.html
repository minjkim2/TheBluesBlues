LATIN POP MUSIC

Latin Music have been around since 16th century. Latin Music originated in parts of Europe, Central and South America, and the Caribbeans. It was influenced by the beats in traditional African Music. There are many styles of Latin Music (i.e. son cubano, rumba, salsa, merengue, tango, samba, bossa nova, etc.). Popular Latin Pop artists include Enrique Iglesias, Prince Royce, Shakira, Pitbull, Chino y Nacho and many more.
