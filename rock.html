ROCK MUSIC

Rock Music has been around for a while, it originated as rock and roll during the late 1940's and the beginning of the 1950's. In the 1960s, it developed into different styles of Rock Music. Rock Music wasn't created by one person; it was a mixture of music by Fats Domino, Ike Turner, Hank Williams, Ray Charles, Jerry Lee Lewis, Bo Diddley, Buddy Holly, Elvis Presley and others. Rock Music originated and evolved in the United states. There are different styles of rock including: Acid rock, Art punk, Bandana Thrash, Big beat, Death rock, Instrumental rock, Metalcore, etc.
