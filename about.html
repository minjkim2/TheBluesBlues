ABOUT US

Who are The Blue Blues? We’re a nonprofit website designed to give children the resources to learn about art and music history. Art classes are underfunded in schools, and both art and music classes fail to teach the history of the subject. We want to provide children with a beginning bit of information to inspire them to learn more.

Why the Blue Blues? If Picasso had a blue period, we can too. Blue is an important color, used to convey emotions, both happy and sad. It’s scientifically proven to be a calming color. Blues as a genre of music is emotional and rich with history. Art and music are both heavily influenced by the blues, whether it be the color or the genre, and we’re influenced by art and music.
