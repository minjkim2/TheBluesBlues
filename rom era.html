ROMANTIC ERA: WHAT IS IT?

The Romantic Era began in 1830 and ended in 1900. The music from this period has the same components as the music in the Classical period, except that it is more expressive and intense. It was started by Beethoven, who ironically ended the Classical Era. This time period was specific to the West. During this time composers made music that reflected more on their emotions unlike before when compsers made music that pleased the people and the church.

Famous Composers During this Era

  • Claude Debussy

    (De-buss-ee) 1862-1918
    He started the modern section of classical music.

  • Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky

    (Chii-cough-ski) 1840-1893
    Without him we wouldn't have the Nutcracker, Sleeping Beauty, and Swan Lake as ballets.

  • Frederic Chopin

    (Show-pan) 1810-1849
    He started to write music when he was seven years old.

  • Johannes Brahms

    (Brarms) 1833-1897
    He spent over 20 years writing just one symphony.

  • Richard Wagner

    (Varg-ner) 1813-1883
    He mainly wrote operas.
