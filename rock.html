Rock Music

Rock Music has been around for a while now, it originated as rock and roll during the late 1940s and the beginning of 1950, and in the 1960s it developed into different styles of Rock Music. Rock Music wasn't really created by one person, it was a black and white alloy of Fats Domino ( born on February 26, 1928), Lloyd Price (born on March 9, 1933), Ike Turner (November 5, 1931 - December 12, 2007), Hank Williams (September 17, 1923 - January 1, 1953), Joe Turner (May 18, 1911 – November 24, 1985), Louis Jordan (July 8, 1908 – February 4, 1975), Ray Charles (September 23, 1930 – June 10, 2004), Little Richard (born December 5, 1932), Jerry Lee Lewis (born September 29, 1935), Chuck Berry (born October 18, 1926), Bo Diddley ( December 30, 1928 – June 2, 2008), Buddy Holly (September 7, 1936 – February 3, 1959), and Elvis Presley (January 8, 1935 – August 16, 1977). It is known that Rock Music originated and evolved in the United states.
