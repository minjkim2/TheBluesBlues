BAROQUE ERA: WHAT IS IT?

The name Baroque is pronounced Ba-Rock. It started in the early 1600's and ended when the Classical Period started to take over which is the mid 1700's. Baroque is not a period of time; instead, it was a style of music. There are two commonly used features wich are: quick back and forward motions on a single note and a lot of harmony. During this time preiod, religion was a huge part of the music made, but slowly the music became more petic and emotional.
