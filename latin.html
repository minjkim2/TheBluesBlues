Latin Music

Latin Music have been around for a very long time, it has been around since 16th century. Latin Music originated in Romance-speaking countries and territories of the Americas and the Caribbean south of the United States. It was also influenced by African music from the traditions and beats.Latin music is a musical category that hold within music from the Spanish-speaking areas of the world (namely Latin America and Spain). Latin music also include Portuguese-language music from Brazil and sometimes Portugal. There are many styles of Latin Music some include son cubano, rumba, salsa, merengue, tango, samba, bossa nova, etc. some popular Latin Artist include Enrique Iglesias, Prince Royce
