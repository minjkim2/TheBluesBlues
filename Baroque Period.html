BAROQUE ERA: WHAT IS IT?

The name Baroque is pronounced Ba-Rock. It started in the early 1600's and ended when the Classical Period started to take over in the mid 1700's. Baroque is not a period of time; instead, it was a style of music. There are two commonly used features; quick back-and-forth motions on a single note and a lot of harmony. During this time preiod, religion was a huge part of the music made, but slowly the music became more poetic and emotional.

Famous Composers During this Era

  • George Frideric Handel

    (handle) 1685–1759
    He was the official composer for King George the first.

  • Johann Sebastian Bach

    (bark) 1685–1750
    He wrote over 1000 pieces of music (that's a lot).

  • Antonio Vivaldi

    (Vi-val-dee) 1678-1741
    He wrote over 500 concertos and 46 operas.

  • Johann Pachelbel

    (Pack-el-bell) 1653-1706
    He is only known for one of his music, Canon in D.

  • Harpsichord

    Although this is not a composer, this instrument was widely used during this time period.
