LATIN POP MUSIC

Latin Music have been around since 16th century. Latin Music originated in the romance-speaking countries, and the territories of Central and South America and the Caribbean. It was influenced by the beats in traditional African Music. Latin music is a musical category that hold within music from the Spanish-speaking areas of the world (i.e. Latin America and Spain). Latin music also includes Portuguese-languaged music from Brazil and Portugal. There are many styles of Latin Music(e.g. son cubano, rumba, salsa, merengue, tango, samba, bossa nova, etc.). Popular Latin Pop Artist include Enrique Iglesias, Prince Royce, Shakira, Pitbull, and more.
