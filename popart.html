POP ART

Who: Andy Warhol, Roy Lichtenstein

What: Paintings and sculptures of culture objects and media stars, usually bright and colorful

When: Mid 1950's to Early 1970's

Where: Britain and the United States

Why: Pop art aimed to blur the lines between "high class" art and "low class" pop culture

EXAMPLES

    Roy Lichtenstein, 1963

  • Standard Station

    David Hockney, 1967

  • Thinking of Him

    Roy Lichtenstein, 1963
